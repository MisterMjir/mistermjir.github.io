Discography

A probably non-comprehensive list of the music I've worked on in reverse chronological order.

A Few Little Things

EP 2025

A lofi-ish EP of some random tracks I put together. They are just a few chill tracks which I made just to keep myself busy tbh.

My Hero's Journey

Album 2025

An album about the hero's journey, like what you learned in English class a long time ago once. This is my second album, and my first album featuring electronic music that's not solely chiptune.

Unconventional Lobby

Single 2025

I sampled some bucket drumming one day and turned it into a lofi jazzy track. Personally, I think it's really good so I just released it as a single.

Blip Bop Baloongazona

EP 2025

I got a job and was able to buy FL studio to start experimenting with new sounds! This is a collection of singles I made while messing around and learning FL studio. Not really a main release, but I released it anyways for fun.

Lost Tracks from '22

EP 2025

Some old tracks I found that were made on Garage Band on an iPad in 2022. Most are just released as is. Also I guess they are no longer lost lol.

alphabit_soup

Album 2023

A collection of 26 chiptunes all made with PxTone. PxTone is a free music software created by the guy who made the video game Cavestory. The first song was released on June 25, 2021 and last on June 25, 2023. Actually a pure coincidence it's on the same day but years apart. My debut album I guess? You can find the full album on my Bandcamp or any major music streaming service.
